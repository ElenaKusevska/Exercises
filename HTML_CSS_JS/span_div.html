What's the deal with span in html?

The span tag can be used to style a smaller section of the text within an element/tag of the HTML document. For example see: https://www.w3schools.com/tags/tag_span.asp

This kinda makes sense if we consider the meaning of span in the English language. Links to definitions of span in some of the most popular online dictionaries are listed below:

  • Merriam-Webster
  • Cambridge Dictionary
  • Collins English Dictionary

What about div?

Similarly to span , div can also be used to style a section of text in an html. The difference is that div typically wraps around several tags/elements within in the html that the style in question will be applied to, wherease span is used on a section of text within one tag, for example the p tag, or the h tag
